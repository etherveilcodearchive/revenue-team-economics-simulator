How to use this workspace

Build the plan in order.

  1. Anchor the business decision.
  2. Choose only relevant revenue motions.
  3. Enter assumptions, then read calculated economics.
  4. Select the plan, ask the analyst, and preserve the artifact.
1

Set decision context

Define what this plan must decide.

The final artifact is only useful if the planning question is explicit before numbers start moving.

2

Choose revenue motions

Decide what belongs in the model.

Inactive motions are intentionally excluded from economics and the final plan.

3

Tune assumptions

Adjust volume, cost, and conversion.

This is the input workbench. Recommendations stay out until the analyst stage.

4

Review economics

Read the calculated relationships.

The simulator shows facts: profit, CAC, funnel movement, and audit rows.
